Who am I?

I am someone who always thought 'how does that work?' How does Google magically index millions of websites every day? Why does that game look so good? This is what originally inspired me to become a programmer.

I decided to drop out of Year 11 VCE and persue more worthwhile education. I completed the Certificate IV of IT at Chisholm and moved onto the Diploma of IT in Swinburne which is what I'm studying now.

During my time away from high school, I went on to learn ASP.NET and have made serveral small-scale APIs and websites which you can find bellow. I ultimately ended up really enjoying backend development, and picked up frontend web development along the way

Throughout my career, I wish to learn more web frameworks like NodeJS, ReactJS, Vue, and others that have always caught my interest, but never had the time to learn them.
